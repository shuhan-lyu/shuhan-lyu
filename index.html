Eagles-2022: Sculpting by oneself

Illustration in 'Yi Lin', 2022.

It is about an inspirational story. The sculptor carved an eagle from an ordinary stone. And his point of view was philosophical. 'There was already an eagle in the stone. I just removed the excess part, and it flew up.'

What is the excess part of a self? It is vanity beyond ability. It is greed beyond personality. The real growth is to drive the force of vita, to form the true value of self and to pursue the duty of life. The stone eagle would never fly into the sky, but it stood with its duty. The small step Neil Armstrong stepped out on moon, stepped out an era of mankind.

Hence, the excess part is too much thought other than resolution, too much behavior other than concentration, and even what to pursue other than what to give up.

Eagles-2020:Prancing manada

Illustration in 'Yi Lin', 2020.

The writer is a descendant of Genghis Khan, who spent his childhood at the endless prairie. Prancing manada is one of the spectacular sceneries in prairie. Different from a solitary horse, a herd of horses are the 'Eagles of the Grassland', who have much more powerful, dexterous, and beautiful.of animal intelligence than human being. The strong emotion from his heart is full of love and awe to nature.

This evokes my addicted feeling on the galloping lead horse there. It is unique because of the abundant field, oxygen, and the realm between sky and earth.

When you blend into this huge group, you will no longer afraid of the eagle passing overhead. You will understand  the very close connection between the environment, and  the coming into being of the pastureland culture, the spatial behavior and the pastureland nationalities.

'Speech and Eloquence',2021: Reindeer

Illustration in 'Speech and Eloquence',2021.

The writer perceives the change of times, like the relationship between Santa and reindeer. Santa Claus should be a fairy beloved by all children. When I was a elementary freshman in Orrington, I admired the yearly 2-workday Santa. But reindeer keep marching a year. Because they live in the extreme cold of the Arctic Circle, food scarcity is a major problem. A reindeer travels about five thousand kilometers a year in search of food.

The writer Zhaozheng Xiong is a famous contemporary writer and poet. He is a committee member of China Federation of Literature and Arts, and the chairman of Hubei Federation of Literature and Arts. Through this story, he recalled his experiences living with the Ewenki people of China, the immortals among the 56 ethnic groups.The days was like the ancient envy of immortal life, making freinds with reindeer and twilight. The more so comfortable, the more memorable, the more he worries about his reindeer.
